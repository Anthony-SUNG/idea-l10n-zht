Reports calls to readLine() that can be replaced with readln() or readlnOrNull().

使用相應函數可使程式碼更簡單。

The quick-fix replaces readLine()!! with readln() and readLine() with readlnOrNull().

範例：


    val x = readLine()!!
    val y = readLine()?.length

在套用快速修復後：


    val x = readln()
    val y = readlnOrNull()?.length
